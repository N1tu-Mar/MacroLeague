01 Who we are

MacroLeague is a mobile and web app that lets you log meals, track nutrition targets, and compete with friends on leaderboards and challenges. This policy covers the MacroLeague app on iOS, Android, and the web, along with the accounts and data behind it.

If you have any question about your privacy or want to exercise a right described below, email us at nityanth.maramreddy@gmail.com. That address is also our support contact.

02 Information we collect

We only collect what the app needs to work and to get better. That falls into a few groups:

  • Account informationWhen you sign up we store your email address, and — if you use Sign in with Apple or Google — the identifier and email that provider returns. If you use Apple's Hide My Email, we only ever see the private relay address. Your display name and profile avatar are stored when you set them.
  • Profile & goalsNutrition goals, macro targets, and preferences you enter during onboarding and in settings.
  • Nutrition & meal dataThe meals you log, their estimated nutrition, timestamps, and your resulting daily scores and streaks.
  • Social & league dataFriend connections, challenge and league participation, and the leaderboard standing that other members in your leagues can see.
  • Usage & diagnosticsAnonymous or pseudonymous data about how you use the app — screens visited, actions taken, performance, and crash reports — collected to keep the app stable and to improve it. See section 4.

03 How we use your information

We use the information above to:

  • Create and secure your account and sign you in.
  • Run the core product — logging meals, calculating scores, and tracking progress.
  • Power social features, including friends, challenges, and leaderboards.
  • Send account-related messages such as password resets and deletion notices.
  • Understand how the product is used so we can fix problems and improve it over time.

04 Analytics & crash reporting

MacroLeague uses Sentry to record crashes and errors, and TelemetryDeck and Amplitude to record product-usage analytics. Together these tell us where the app breaks and how people actually move through it — for example, how far new users get through onboarding, or where meal logging slows down — so we can prioritize fixes and improvements. Product-analytics events include actions such as completing onboarding, logging your first meal, starting a session, joining a challenge, and viewing rewards.

By creating an account and accepting our Terms of Service, you consent to your usage and analytics data being used to operate and improve MacroLeague as we keep building it. This is a standard practice for consumer apps and is described in plain terms here and in the Terms.

We aim to keep analytics tied to a pseudonymous identifier rather than to directly identifying details, and we do not use it to sell you to anyone.

05 When we share data

We do not sell your personal data. We share it only in these limited ways:

  • Service providersWe rely on infrastructure partners that process data on our behalf under their own terms: Supabase (database, authentication, and storage), Sentry (crash and error reporting), TelemetryDeck and Amplitude (product-usage analytics), and Apple and Google (sign-in and app distribution).
  • Other membersYour display name, avatar, and league standing are visible to other members of leagues and challenges you join. Your email and meal details are never shown to them.
  • Legal reasonsIf required by law, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of our users and the service.

06 Data retention & deletion

You can request deletion of your account at any time from inside the app, under account settings. Deletion is designed to be reversible for a short window in case you change your mind:

  • Soft deleteWhen you request deletion, your account is immediately archived and hidden, and we schedule it for permanent removal after a 14-day recovery window.
  • ReactivationIf you sign back in during that window, you can cancel the deletion and restore your account and data.
  • Permanent purgeAfter the window passes, a scheduled server-side job permanently deletes your account and associated data.

We otherwise keep your data for as long as your account is active. You may also email nityanth.maramreddy@gmail.com to request deletion or ask a question about retention.

07 Your choices & rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. You can update most of your information directly in the app, delete your account in settings, and contact us for anything else. We will not discriminate against you for exercising these rights.

08 Security

Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS). Access to your records is enforced by row-level security so that, in normal operation, you can only read and change your own data. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to protect your information and to limit what any one party can access.

09 Age requirement

MacroLeague is intended for users aged 13 and older, and is built primarily for a college and young-adult audience. It is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their data. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us and we will remove it.

10 Changes & contact

We may update this policy as the product evolves. When we make a material change, we will update the effective date above and, where appropriate, notify you in the app. Continued use after an update means you accept the revised policy.
