01 Who we are

MacroLeague is a mobile and web app that lets you log meals, track nutrition targets, earn points and streaks, and compete with friends on leaderboards and challenges. This policy covers the MacroLeague app on iOS, Android, and the web, along with the accounts and data behind it. In this policy, “MacroLeague,” “we,” “us,” and “our” refer to the operator of MacroLeague, who acts as the controller of your personal data.

If you have any question about your privacy or want to exercise a right described below, email us at nityanth.maramreddy@gmail.com. That address is also our support contact.

02 Information we collect

We only collect what the app needs to work and to get better. That falls into a few groups:

  • Account informationWhen you sign up we store your email address, and — if you use Sign in with Apple or Google — the identifier and email that provider returns. If you use Apple’s Hide My Email, we only ever see the private relay address. Your username, display name, and profile avatar are stored when you set them.
  • Profile & goalsNutrition goals, macro targets (calories, protein, carbs, fats), your time zone, and preferences you enter during onboarding and in settings.
  • Nutrition & meal dataThe meals you log, their descriptions and estimated nutrition, timestamps, and the daily scores, streaks, XP, and points that result. This is the core of what the app does.
  • Social & league dataFriend connections and requests, challenge and league participation, activity-feed events, reactions, any social handles you choose to add (such as Instagram, Snapchat, or TikTok), and the leaderboard standing that other members in your leagues can see.
  • Rewards dataThe points you spend, rewards you redeem, and the redemption codes issued to your account.
  • Device & notification dataIf you enable notifications, we store a push token for your device so we can deliver them. We also process basic technical data such as app version, device type, and operating system.
  • Usage & diagnosticsPseudonymous data about how you use the app — screens visited, actions taken, performance, and crash reports — collected to keep the app stable and to improve it. See sections 5.

03 How we use your information

We use the information above to:

  • Create and secure your account and sign you in.
  • Run the core product — logging meals, estimating nutrition, calculating scores, and tracking progress.
  • Power social features, including friends, challenges, leaderboards, and the rewards catalog.
  • Deliver push notifications you have enabled, and send account-related messages such as password resets and account-deletion notices.
  • Prevent cheating, fraud, and abuse, and enforce our Terms of Service.
  • Understand how the product is used so we can fix problems and improve it over time.

04 AI features & nutrition data

MacroLeague includes AI-assisted features, and it draws on public nutrition databases. Here is exactly what that means for your data.

  • AI meal estimationWhen you describe a meal in words, we send that description to our AI provider, OpenAI, to turn it into an estimated nutrition breakdown. We send the meal text — not your name or email.
  • MacroCoach (AI nutrition coach)When you chat with MacroCoach, your messages are sent to OpenAI to generate a reply. To personalize answers, we also include limited context such as your first name or athlete label, your goals, and your progress for the day. Please don’t share sensitive personal or medical details in chat that you wouldn’t want processed by an AI provider.
  • Nutrition data sourcesTo look up foods we query public nutrition databases — the U.S. Department of Agriculture FoodData Central and Open Food Facts — and cache results to speed up future lookups.

OpenAI processes this data as our service provider to return a result to you. We do not use your meals or coach conversations to train our own models, and we rely on OpenAI’s API terms, under which API data is not used to train their models by default. AI output is an estimate and can be wrong — see the health section below.

05 Analytics & crash reporting

MacroLeague uses Sentry to record crashes and errors, and TelemetryDeck and Amplitude to record product-usage analytics. Together these tell us where the app breaks and how people actually move through it — for example, how far new users get through onboarding, or where meal logging slows down — so we can prioritize fixes and improvements. Product-analytics events include actions such as completing onboarding, logging your first meal, starting a session, joining a challenge, and viewing rewards.

By creating an account and accepting our Terms of Service, you consent to your usage and analytics data being used to operate and improve MacroLeague as we keep building it. This is a standard practice for consumer apps and is described in plain terms here and in the Terms.

We tie analytics to a pseudonymous identifier rather than to directly identifying details, we do not include your email in analytics events, and we do not sell you to anyone.

06 When we share data

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising. We disclose it only in these limited ways:

  • Service providers (sub-processors)We rely on trusted partners that process data on our behalf under their own terms and only to provide their service to us: Supabase (database, authentication, and storage); OpenAI (AI meal estimation and coach); USDA FoodData Central and Open Food Facts (food lookups); Sentry (crash and error reporting); TelemetryDeck and Amplitude (product-usage analytics); Expo (push-notification delivery); Resend (transactional email); and Apple and Google (sign-in and app distribution).
  • Other membersYour username, display name, avatar, league standing, activity-feed events, and any social handles you add are visible to other members of leagues and challenges you join, or to your accepted friends. Your email address and the specific contents and macros of your meals are never shown to them.
  • Legal & safetyIf required by law, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of our users and the service.
  • Business transferIf MacroLeague is ever involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to this policy.

07 Health & nutrition data

Meal logs, macros, and goals describe your eating and can feel sensitive, so we treat them with care: they stay private to your account, they are protected by row-level access rules, and they are never shown to other members.

MacroLeague is a fitness and nutrition-tracking tool, not a medical device or medical service, and its estimates and scores are for informational and motivational purposes only — not medical or dietary advice. If food, weight, or restriction feel like they’re becoming harmful, please reach out to a qualified professional. In the U.S. you can contact the National Alliance for Eating Disorders helpline at 1-866-662-1235.

08 Data retention & deletion

You can request deletion of your account at any time from inside the app, under account settings. Deletion is designed to be reversible for a short window in case you change your mind:

  • Soft deleteWhen you request deletion, your account is immediately archived and hidden, and we schedule it for permanent removal after a 14-day recovery window.
  • ReactivationIf you sign back in during that window, you can cancel the deletion and restore your account and data.
  • Permanent purgeAfter the window passes, a scheduled server-side job permanently deletes your account and associated data.

We otherwise keep your data for as long as your account is active. Some records may be retained longer where necessary to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or prevent abuse. You may also email nityanth.maramreddy@gmail.com to request deletion or ask a question about retention.

09 Your privacy rights

You can update most of your information directly in the app, delete your account in settings, and contact us for anything else. We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights. Depending on where you live, you may also have specific rights:

  • EEA & UK (GDPR)You have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, and port your data, and to object to certain processing. Our legal bases are performance of our contract with you (to run the app), your consent (for optional features such as notifications), and our legitimate interests (to secure and improve the service). You may withdraw consent at any time and can complain to your local data protection authority.
  • California (CCPA/CPRA)You have the right to know what personal information we collect and how we use it, to request deletion or correction, and to opt out of “sale” or “sharing” — which we do not do. We do not sell or share personal information as those terms are defined under California law.
  • Everyone elseWherever you are, you can access, correct, or delete your data by using the in-app controls or emailing us.

To make a request, email nityanth.maramreddy@gmail.com. We may need to verify your identity before acting.

10 International data transfers

11 Cookies & local storage

The MacroLeague apps and website use device storage and similar technologies only for essential purposes — keeping you signed in and remembering your settings. We do not use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies.

12 Security

Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS). Access to your records is enforced by row-level security so that, in normal operation, you can only read and change your own data, and sensitive server keys are never shipped in the app. Passwords are handled by our authentication provider and are never stored by us in readable form. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to protect your information and to limit what any one party can access.

13 Age requirement

MacroLeague is intended for users aged 13 and older, and is built primarily for a college and young-adult audience. It is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their data. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us and we will remove it. Some jurisdictions require a higher minimum age or parental consent; where that applies, you must meet those requirements to use MacroLeague.
