MacroLeague — light-first redesign
Screen inventory · design foundations · all major states · July 2026
Assumptions & decisions
Light-first per the brief: bg #F5F6F3, ink #171A1F, scarlet #D9364A as the single brand accent (deep carmine #A8141E for pressed states, bridging the current app). DM Sans for interface copy, Barlow Condensed reserved for competitive numerals — scores, ranks, LP, countdowns. Gold appears only in Rewards and 1st place. Lucide icons, kept from the current icon system. Pixel flame kept as the one intentional game-art exception.
Rationale (borrowed / avoided / distinct)
Borrowed: Cal AI's one-dominant-status hierarchy and honest estimate review; MyFitnessPal's labeled forms. Avoided: macro ring grids, gradient heroes, glass cards, sparkle-AI iconography, equal-card dashboards. Distinct: the league standing treated as a real sports table, condensed score typography, and the meal→XP→LP→rank loop made visible at every confirmation.

25Scoring rules + Notifications
How scoring works
XP
XP levels you up. It only ever grows and never resets. Your level and title live on your profile.
LP
League Points rank you. They count within the current league window and reset when it ends. Spend them in Rewards.
Each confirmed meal earns 50 XP and 10 LP. Editing a meal doesn't earn again.
Bonuses: +5 LP per challenge goal day, +40 LP for a challenge win, +20 LP each 7-day streak.
